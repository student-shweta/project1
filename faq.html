FAQs

How is the MBA Program of ISMTS different from other MBA Program?

The MBA Program focuses on knowledge and skills required by management professionals who plan to work for Indian and multinational companies. It provides the students with a general management perspective and introduces them to the latest concepts and practices related to functional areas of management. The substantive exposure to Indian and international management practices with a strong case-study orientation is a unique feature of the MBA Program. In addition, the MBA Program is structured in a flexible manner so that working executives can pursue this program without disturbing their careers.

Is there any Admission Test for enrolling into any Programme(s) of ISMTS

No, there is no admission test for enrolling into any Programme(s) of ISMTS. These programs are open to all those applicants who satisfy the eligibility criteria as indicated.

I am a working executive. Can I pursue any Program on a part-time basis?

Yes, any Program can be pursued on a self-study basis. You can do self-study in your free time, and appear for the examinations and complete the program. It is designed completely in a flexible manner to suit the needs of the working executives.

Are the MBA and other Programmes of ISMTS recognized?

In the changing economic environment what matters most is the recognition by the employers/industry. The MBA Program is highly appreciated and valued by blue-chip employers, as it is a uniquely designed program in Management, comprehensively covering all the functional areas of management in great depth. In today’s buyers’ market, these professionals will have good prospects in Indian companies and also in MNCs operating in India and abroad. ISMTS is a non-university institution. All programmes of ISMTS are autonomous and do not come under the preview of UGC/AICTE. ISMTS do not award degree.

What are the placement prospects for the MBA’s?

Acquiring the MBA qualification will give a sustainable competitive advantage for candidates who are interested in career opportunities with progressive employers. The MBA Program offers a broad spectrum of management courses with a strong practical orientation through its emphasis on case-based learning. The candidates who pursue the MBA Program will get exposed to a cutting-edge curriculum. Thus, successful candidates will be able to explore career opportunities with leading Indian and multinational companies.

What placement assistance does the ISMTS offer to me?

ISMTS believes that the entire placement exercise is a joint effort between ISMTS and candidates. ISMTS provides guidance to its students in their placement efforts. We do not invite companies for campus recruitment.

Are the courses offered by ISMTS is equivalent to PUC or any Degree?

No. Since ISMTS is a self autonomous body and also not affiliated by any UGC or AICTE, its certification will not be equivalent to any PUC or Degree.

If I have any further questions whom should I contact?

If you have any further questions, please email us on “info@ismts.in“
