WELCOME TO ISMTS

Indian School of Management and Technical Studies (ISMTS) is an autonomous institute of excellence offering wide range of programmes in the field of management and technical education. Indian School of Management And Technical Studies (ISMTS) is registered by “Indian Council For Management Studies” [ICMS] and “Indian Council For Engineering and Technical Studies” [ICETS].

ISMTS offer potential students the opportunity to study and transform into outstanding citizens. The programs offered by ISMTS are well planned and designed based on in depth analysis and research with emphasis on knowledge assimilation, practical applications, global and Industrial relevance and their social significance.. All courses offered by ISMTS are independent programmes and is not affiliated to any university and do not have an approval from statutory bodies like AICTE, UGC or DEC. These studies also help the students to aquint themselves with the latest developments emerging in business environment and also enhance the prospects of their career.

As education grows and diversifies, ISMTS safeguard standards and support the improvement of quality for students. The scale, shape, structure and purpose of learning provision are changing around the world, ISMTS is uniquely placed to anticipate and respond to these changes in order to safeguard the reputation of education, support, economic opportunity and provide assurance to those who undertake learning.

The courses offered by ISMTS in Management and Technical education are extensively designed to meet the special needs of entrepreneurs, businessmen and professionals. These studies equip the students to formulate various strategies and manage their business efficiently. The studies also help the students to aquint themselves with the latest developments emerging in business environment and also enhance the prospects of their career. It is a matter of great pride that the excellent quality of Course material, Web Support, delivery methodology and student support services have been recognized. ISMTS aims to provide quality education in basic/specialized/advance areas of management including Finance, Human Resource Development, Software Marketing, Marketing, Sales, Tourism, and Insurance Management etc. The courses are designed to develop business leaders by nurturing knowledge, skills, attitudes and behavior.

Indian School of Management and Technical Studies (ISMTS) is a non-profit making institute which offers comprehensive autonomous educational programmes to train and develop a new generation of entrepreneurs to face with confidence the fluid global challenges of national / international market and get them intellectually enriched. The Institute provides all necessary educational and administrative support so that the students can successfully complete their respective course of study.

ISMTS strives to maintain an environment which encourages all students to achieve their personal and professional goals. ISMTS recognizes the importance of communication and is committed to an environment which stresses open sharing of information and ideas and values input from all students. ISMTS will strive for an environment in which all students accept responsibility to contribute to the success of the institute, and are empowered to do so. Finally, for this vision to become reality and endure, it must be continually communicated, supported and upheld.

ISMTS provides innovative programs in Management and Technical education for students in an environment that supports economic development, professional development and involvement with businesses and industries to meet the special needs of entrepreneurs, businessmen and professionals.

Indian School of Management And Technical Studies (ISMTS) strives to :

  • Produce – we produce high-quality programmes through our Learning model.
  • Innovate – we explore innovative approaches that stimulate thinking, enhance the learning experience and contribute to improving student’s career.
  • Research – we promote and support fundamental and applied research that enhances scientific discovery and fosters the growth of all students.
  • Invest – we invest in developing and retaining outstanding team members to accomplish our mission.
  • Teach – we seek to enhance educational effectiveness through continuous improvement and advocacy of original teaching pedagogies.
