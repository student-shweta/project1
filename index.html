ABOUT US

Indian School of Management and Technical Studies (ISMTS) is an autonomous institute of excellence offering wide range of programmes in the field of
management and technical education. Indian School of Management And Technical Studies (ISMTS) is registered by “Indian Council For Management Studies”
[ICMS] and “Indian Council For Engineering and Technical Studies” [ICETS]
